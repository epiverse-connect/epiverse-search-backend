# Coerce and store `<data.frame>` subclass to `<data.frame>` and restore `<data.frame>` subclass to `<data.frame>` from attribute.

```r
.as_df(x)

.restore_df_subclass(x)
```

## Arguments

- `x`: An object.

## Returns

A `<data.frame>` or subclass of `<data.frame>`.

Coerce and store `<data.frame>` subclass to `<data.frame>` and restore `<data.frame>` subclass to `<data.frame>` from attribute.
